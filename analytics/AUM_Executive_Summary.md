## The Game

**AUM** is a competitive 3D multiplayer fighting game featuring Indian mythology-based combat with:

- **5 Unique Fighting Styles** (melee + ranged)
- **3 Deity Power Systems** (Brahma/Vishnu/Shiva)
- **60Hz Server-Authoritative Netcode** (competitive-grade)
- **Deep Character Customization** (cosmetics, progression, karma)
